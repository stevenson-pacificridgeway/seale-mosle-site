The Entos Approach to Insurance Design

Most agents design for commission. We design for accumulation.

Indexed life insurance is one of the most misunderstood instruments in personal finance. Designed around a commission, a policy can be over-insured relative to premium, weighed down by internal costs, and structured to quietly erode the very cash value it promises to build.

Designed around the strategy, the same instrument behaves entirely differently — funded for maximum accumulation, with minimal insurance cost, working as a genuinely tax-advantaged vehicle. We show you exactly where that difference comes from, in dollars, before you sign anything.

"Every recommendation begins with the strategy and works backward to the instrument. The carrier comes last. The product comes last. Your situation comes first — always."
